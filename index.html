Our Story

MamaLoop began with a mother who spent years working, caring, and creating for others. Somewhere along the way, her love for crochet was paused. Today, she’s picking it up again — slowly, patiently, and with imagination.

A craft rediscovered

Each piece is made by hand — no machines, no rushing. We honor time, skill, and quiet creativity.

A bridge across countries

I manage the brand from afar — website, Instagram, and customer messages — while my mom creates each piece back home with love.
